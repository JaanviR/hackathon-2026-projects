# Responsible AI Documentation

## Training Data Sources
- Anonymized clinical datasets (simulated for hackathon).
- Public medical literature.

## Model Bias Analysis
- Auditing for gender, age, and rural/urban split parity.
- Focus on minimizing false negatives for emergency cases.

## Failure Modes
- Misinterpretation of rare symptoms.
- Dialect variations in Nepali language input.

## Nepali Language Equity
- Support for Romanized Nepali and Devanagari script.
- Bilingual symptom mapping to ensure equal quality of care.

## Misuse Scenarios
- Intentional false symptom reporting.
- Relying on the tool for life-threatening emergencies without contacting official emergency services.
